Description

With "WER WEISS DENN SOWAS?" – the official game based on one of Germany’s most successful TV show formats – you can now put your knowledge to the test. Play alongside Elton, Bernhard Hoëcker, or up to three friends in exciting contests. 30 complete shows with over 390 questions from more than 100 categories and the corresponding answers await you. The gripping matches will demand everything you’ve got. You’ll often be left wondering “Who even knows these things?” – or do you know what the 20-20-20 rule helps with? No?

  • Perfect implementation of the TV show with the characters and original voices of Kai Pflaume, Bernhard Hoëcker, and Elton.
  • 30 complete shows with over 390 questions from more than 100 categories, and answer videos just like in the show itself.
  • One, two, three, and four-player modes
  • You choose who you play with – alongside Bernhard Hoëcker, on Team Elton, or perhaps you’d prefer to play together with your friends?
  • Create your own character using a variety of different options
  • Check your game statistics regularly and compare them with your friends’ – who has won the most money for their viewers?
