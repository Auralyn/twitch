Just Updated



The first part of the seasonal update has been installed on the main servers. We have prepared for you a new contract system, the "Ruins" map for the "Capture" mode, a ton of weaponry novelties, thematic sets, equipment and skins, a Battle Pass and much more. Meet the "Hidden War" with guns blazing, get together friends and explore the new content!



There is a new contract system, which is completely different from the previously existing mechanics. The updated contracts work very simply:

  • in the main menu and the lobby, the player goes to the "Contracts" section (available on the top panel);
  • a special menu allows you to see all the available contracts and information about them (execution conditions, activation costs, progress status and reward upon completion);
  • some contracts can have a certain fee (for example, WF$), but the reward for their completion is higher;
  • there are two types of contracts: a one-time set of tasks or a sequential chain of them;
  • a player may have an unlimited number of active contracts at the same time.
  • some contracts have time constraints; offers like those may disappear at any time.



The new map for the "Capture" mode will greet you with a tropical climate and the possibility to use a variety of strategies. The confrontation between Blackwood and Warface will unfold in hot Africa among the rubble of the ancient ruins, which have prepared some treacherous surprises for those who dare explore them.



The appearance of the new series will satisfy even the most discerning gun connoisseurs. They have a stylish design, great variability of patterns, as well their key feature, unique eye-catching elements inherent in every model that will certainly not leave you indifferent. Some guns have exclusive external elements that enhance the usual models and give them a fresh coat of paint. Check out how cool they are!



The frames of the models from the new series are decorated with a gold pattern using geometric motifs. It resembles a scratch card for travelers that you need to scratch out if you’ve visited certain places in the world. You also have a difficult journey ahead 一 so grab the new guns to learn about the new game content with them!



A new special set of "Hidden War" gear has appeared in the seasonal update. The pack includes a helmet and a vest for all classes (except for SEDs). The specs of the new set are the same for each class. The "Hidden War" gear will allow you to diversify equipment to fit different needs of your fighter, combining new elements with the already existing ones.



Have you been waiting to give your opponent a proper beating, but doing that with a toy hammer was a bit too difficult? No problem! Now we have some serious novelty 一 the bat. You don’t have to shy away from the appearance of the weapon. Just equip the bat and demonstrate the most serious intentions in a fight! The weapon is available in several variants, which differ from each other substantially. In addition, we have prepared some camouflages for the new arm.



Just one look at this novelty, will probably remind you of a couple of popular films about the not so friendly guests from other planets and dimensions. The frame of the models from the new series is styled in dark colours using contrasting green inserts. They shine beautifully, as if filling the weapon with unearthly power. Give in to the temptation — bring these guns into your next battle!

About the Game

Warface is a contemporary MMO first person shooter with millions of fans around the world. It offers intense PvP modes, compelling PvE missions and raids that you can challenge with five diverse classes and a colossal customizable arsenal.

Intense PvP


Over ten multiplayer modes with dozens of maps – from time proven shooter classics to fresh experiments, as well the elaborated system of ranked matches with unique rewards await its heroes.

Compelling PvE


Play with your friends in exciting online missions or try yourself in full-scale raids with several difficulty levels and epic loot.

Diverse classes


Rifleman, Medic, Engineer, Sniper and SED are at your service: assault the defenses of your enemies, revive your teammates, mine the corridors, take down the foes with precise shots or tank the enemy's counter-attack - here you decide how to act!

Enormous customizable arsenal


Hundreds of weapons are at your disposal, customizable with a range of scopes, grips, flash guards, and camo to get the best performance tailored to your preference.

Jump into action and play for free!
